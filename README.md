# Angel-oscuro-001
Project Ángel Oscuro 001
Original Concept by: Dilan A. Lopez Technical Development: Collaboration with AI (Gemini)

1. Introduction: The "Old Space" Problem
Current space exploration is stuck in a cycle of high costs due to reliance on single-use or low-efficiency chemical rockets. Ángel Oscuro 001 proposes an alternative based on aerodynamic efficiency and nuclear electric propulsion (NEP) to drastically reduce the cost of access to deep space.

2. Spacecraft Architecture: Variable Geometry
The design breaks away from traditional "tube" rocket shapes and is divided into two physical phases:

• Atmospheric Phase (Flying Wing Mode): Inspired by B-2 Spirit aerodynamics. It uses total lift surface to rise "bit by bit" through the atmosphere, leveraging air for altitude rather than fighting gravity with brute force.

• Space Phase (Dart Mode): Once in a vacuum, the ship uses a variable geometry mechanism to retract its wings and transform into a compact structure. This eliminates wing instability in a vacuum and allows for precise maneuverability via reaction control systems (RCS).

3. Power System: Atomic Batteries and Plasma Engines
The heart of Ángel Oscuro 001 is not combustion, but nuclear electricity:

• Source: A particle bed reactor (PBR) or high-density radioisotope batteries providing constant power for years.

• Propulsion: Magnetic containment plasma engines.

• Cost Innovation: During ascent, the engine uses an air-breathing system, converting atmospheric air into plasma to save fuel weight. Reserve gas (Xenon or Argon) is only used once in outer space.

4. Economic and Technological Viability
For this project to be possible within this decade, the proposal moves away from unreachable exotic materials and focuses on:

1. Lifting Bodies: Optimized design to reduce mechanical complexity.

2. Digital Twins: Use of supercomputing for millions of stress tests and CFD simulations before building the first prototype.

3. Additive Manufacturing: 3D-printed structure to reduce parts, weight, and human error.

5. Conclusion
Ángel Oscuro 001 is not just a ship; it is a paradigm shift. While the current industry spends on fuel and disposable tanks, this proposal invests in design intelligence and long-lasting energy. It is a vehicle designed to be reusable, scalable, and, above all, logical.
